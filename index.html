About Me

I am currently a student at the University of Central Florida studying Biomechanics and Nanoscale Science. I have a love for all things science, especially space and interdisciplinary topics. More than anything else, I want to help others. I always try to stay involved in community service, and am happiest when my work is directly helping people. My specific academic interests include nanomaterials, medical technology innovation, cancer therapy techniques, siRNA therapies and genetic therapies, and prosthetic design/manufacturing. Those aside, my personal interests often revolve around striving to bake the perfect cinnamon rolls, making my friends laugh, painting, and the quest for the world's best french fries.

UCF Rocketry

I currently serve on a team of students at UCF to build a hybrid motor rocket that will be entered in an international competition. My involvement began in Summer 2019 with contributions to the operations and media subteam, and I now work on the electronics, payload, airframe, propulsion, and finance subteams . The competition we attend each year is the Spaceport America Cup in New Mexico
